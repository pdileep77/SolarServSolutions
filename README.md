Challenges to ensure your Solar Plant is running at full efficiency:
Lack of Professional Infrastructure: There is currently no dedicated, easy-to-use service platform for booking solar panel maintenance and cleaning.
Efficiency Loss: Dirty solar panels can lose up to 25% of their energy output, but homeowners lack a streamlined way to address this.
Safety & Difficulty: DIY cleaning involves climbing roofs and handling electrical equipment, creating a significant barrier for the average user.
Disconnected Market: No centralized system exists to track solar health or connect service providers with system owners.

We help you with on time & cost effective manner to keep your solar system running in best condition.
